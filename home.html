HOW TO PLAY
01
Configure & Launch

Set grid size and pit count with the sliders, then press ⚡ Launch New Mission to deploy the agent at (0,0).

02
Navigate Safely

Move with WASD or Arrow keys. Safe cells glow green; uncertain cells pulse red. Click adjacent cells to move.

03
Read Percepts

🌬 Breeze = pit nearby. 😷 Stench = Wumpus nearby. ✨ Glitter = gold here! Use these to build the KB and prove cells safe.

04
Grab the Gold

When you see Glitter, press G or 💰 Grab Gold. The agent will automatically return to base and you win!

05
Use Auto Step

Let the AI take over — it picks only KB-proven safe moves. Watch the Resolution Steps panel to follow the logic in real time.

06
Reveal the World

Press 👁 Reveal to see all pit and Wumpus positions overlaid on the grid — useful for debugging or showing the full world.
